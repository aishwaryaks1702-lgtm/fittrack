# FitTrack PWA — Deploy Guide

## Your files
- index.html  → main app
- manifest.json → PWA config
- sw.js → offline support
- icons/ → app icons

## Deploy on Netlify (free, 5 minutes)

1. Go to https://netlify.com and sign up free
2. Click "Add new site" → "Deploy manually"
3. Drag and drop the entire fittracker/ folder onto the page
4. Netlify gives you a link like: https://yourname-fittrack.netlify.app
5. Share that link with friends!

## Add to phone home screen

### iPhone (Safari)
1. Open your Netlify link in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow)
3. Scroll down → tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add" → FitTrack icon appears on your home screen!

### Android (Chrome)
1. Open your Netlify link in Chrome
2. Tap the 3-dot menu (top right)
3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
4. Tap "Add" → done!

## Share with friends
Just send them your Netlify link via WhatsApp, Instagram, email — they open it,
add to home screen, and they have the same app!

Each person's data is stored on their own device privately.
